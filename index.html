About CASA

CASA is a student-driven academic and professional committee representing the CO & AN departments. Our mission is to empower students through collaboration, leadership, innovation, and academic excellence.

We organize technical events, academic workshops, peer-learning programs, and student development initiatives to create a strong learning community.
