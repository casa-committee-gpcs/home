About CASA

CO & AN Students Association (CASA) represents the collective academic and professional interests of students from the CO & AN departments. The committee aims to create a strong learning environment by encouraging peer interaction, innovation, and leadership.

CASA organizes academic events, workshops, seminars, peer-learning sessions, and student development initiatives to support holistic growth beyond the classroom.
