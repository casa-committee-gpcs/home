About CASA

CO & AN Students Association (CASA) represents the academic and professional interests of students from the CO & AN departments. The committee works towards building a strong student community through academic events, workshops, and peer-learning initiatives.

CASA aims to support holistic development beyond the classroom and prepare students for future academic and professional challenges.
